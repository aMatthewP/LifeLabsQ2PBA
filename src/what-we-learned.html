Quarter 1

In quarter 1 we learned about YouTube and the different types of content types on YouTube. We also learned about how to appeal and identify to your audience and determine what kind of content we should do.

We planned and created our YouTube channel identifying what type of content we will do and what type of audience do we want to reach.
In quarter 2 we learned how to add JavaScript to our websites and how to use it to make animated features to make our websites more attractive and appealing. Making our sites more dynamic and interactive through the use of JavaScript.

We were able to successfully create a website for our brand for people to view. The website that you are currently viewing is the website that we made in quarter 2.

Quarter 3

In quarter 3 we learned how to plan, create, and edit our videos for our YouTube channel, what we should do before production, during production, and post production. We learned how to use storyboards to plan our video efficiently and cheaply.

Using the information that we learned we made able to plan, create, and produce our first video and successfully uploaded it to YouTube.
In quarter 4 we learned about how to optimize your videos to be picked up by the algorithm through search engine optimization and how to look at, use, and analyze youtube analytics. We also learned how to embed videos into our websites.

This quarter we planned, created, and produced another video for our channel and we were also able to successfully upload it to YouTube. We also embedded the videos we made into the website that we made in quarter 2.
